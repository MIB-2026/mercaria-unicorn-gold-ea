All settings are logically organized into 11 groups. Most users only touch the first two.

▸ Lot Scaling — UseLotScaling, BaseLotReference, GridTPGrowthPercent, VirtualBalanceUSD. Adapting TP/SL/trail to current lot.

▸ Candle Filter — filter by candle direction.

▸ Entries per candle — entries limit per single candle. Protection against over-trading.

▸ Session — TargetProfitUSD (daily target), MaxLossCount (session SL limit). When either is reached, the advisor stops automatically.
